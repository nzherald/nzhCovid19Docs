Explanatory note

This note is not part of the instrument, but is intended to indicate its general
effect.
This instrument, which comes into force on 1 April 2020, amends the Income
Support Package (Transitional Assistance) Programme. It extends the grant of
temporary financial assistance to people who experience an overall reduction in
assistance as a result of the increase to main benefits on 1 April 2020 made by the
COVID-19 Recovery Package. The instrument also corrects minor technical and
formatting errors in the principal programme.
