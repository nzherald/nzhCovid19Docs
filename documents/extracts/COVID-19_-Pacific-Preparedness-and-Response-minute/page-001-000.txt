COVID-19: Pacific Preparedness and Response

Portfolio Foreign Affairs

On 11 March 2020, the Ad Hoc Cabinet Committee on COVID-19 Response, acting in accordance

with its Power to Act:

1 noted that Pacific countries are vulnerable to severe impacts from a COVID-19 outbreak;

2 noted that New Zealand has been working closely with Pacific partners, the World Health
Organisation, and Australia on regional preparedness, but that readiness across the region
remains highly variable;

3

4 agreed that a package of' financial support

social, and governance challenges relating to COVID-19, with:

4.1 of this funding being earmarked to support immediate health system
preparation;

4.2 being used to support broader measures to enable Pacific authorities to
address a range of economic, social and governance constraints;

5 agreed that the Minister of Foreign Affairs be mandated to decide on the allocation of
resources within the envelope, keeping Cabinet updated as the support package
